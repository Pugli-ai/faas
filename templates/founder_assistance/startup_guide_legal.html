Legal & Compliance Guide

Legal compliance is crucial for your startup's success and sustainability in Italy. This guide covers key Italian legal considerations, but it's recommended to consult with commercialisti (business consultants) and legal professionals for specific advice.

1. Choosing a Business Structure

Structure Characteristics Best For Considerations
Ditta Individuale Single owner, simple structure Small, low-risk businesses Personal liability, requires Partita IVA
S.r.l. (Società a responsabilità limitata) Limited liability, flexible management Small to medium businesses Minimum capital €1, common for startups
S.r.l.s. (Semplificata) Simplified S.r.l., lower costs Young entrepreneurs, small startups Capital under €10k, age restrictions
S.p.A. (Società per Azioni) Public limited company Large businesses, listed companies Min. capital €50k, complex governance

2. Registration & Licensing

Essential Registrations

  • Business Registration
  • Partita IVA (VAT Number)
  • Chamber of Commerce Registration (CCIAA)
  • Professional Licenses

Timeline & Costs

  • Business Registration 1-2 weeks
  • Partita IVA 1-2 days
  • CCIAA Registration 5-7 days
  • Municipal Permits 2-4 weeks

3. Intellectual Property Protection

Marchi (Trademarks)

Protect your brand identity:

  • Nome dell'azienda (Company name)
  • Logo
  • Slogan
  • Nomi dei prodotti (Product names)

Brevetti e Diritti d'Autore

Protect your innovations:

  • Invenzioni (Inventions)
  • Codice software (Software code)
  • Contenuti (Content)
  • Design industriale (Designs)

4. Essential Contracts & Agreements

Internal

  • Atto Costitutivo (Articles of Association)
  • Statuto (Company Bylaws)
  • Contratti di Lavoro (Employment Contracts)
  • Accordi di Riservatezza (NDAs)

Customer-Facing

  • Termini di Servizio (Terms of Service)
  • Informativa Privacy (Privacy Policy)
  • Contratti di Vendita (Sales Contracts)
  • Garanzie (Warranties)

Third-Party

  • Accordi con Fornitori (Vendor Agreements)
  • Contratti di Partnership
  • Accordi di Licenza (Licensing)
  • Contratti di Servizio
