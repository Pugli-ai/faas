Legal & Compliance Guide

Legal compliance is crucial for your startup's success and sustainability. This guide covers key legal considerations, but it's recommended to consult with legal professionals for specific advice.

1. Choosing a Business Structure

Structure Characteristics Best For Considerations
Sole Proprietorship Single owner, simple structure Small, low-risk businesses Personal liability, limited funding options
LLC Limited liability, flexible management Small to medium businesses More paperwork, state-specific rules
C-Corporation Separate legal entity, stock options Startups seeking investment Double taxation, complex compliance
S-Corporation Pass-through taxation Small businesses with profits Ownership restrictions, strict rules

2. Registration & Licensing

Essential Registrations

  • Business Registration
  • Tax ID (EIN)
  • State/Local Permits
  • Professional Licenses

Timeline & Costs

  • Business Registration 1-2 weeks
  • EIN Immediate
  • State Licenses 2-6 weeks
  • Local Permits 1-4 weeks
