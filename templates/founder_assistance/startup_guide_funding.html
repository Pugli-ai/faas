Funding & Finance

Understanding your funding options and managing finances effectively is crucial for your startup's success. This guide covers various funding sources and essential financial management practices.

1. Funding Options

Equity Funding

  • Angel Investors

    Individual investors providing early-stage capital

    $25K - $500K
  • Venture Capital

    Professional firms investing in high-growth startups

    $500K - $5M+
  • Crowdfunding

    Raising small amounts from many investors

    $10K - $1M

Debt Funding

  • Bank Loans

    Traditional business loans from banks

    Lower cost, requires collateral
  • SBA Loans

    Government-backed small business loans

    Better terms, longer process
  • Revenue-Based Financing

    Funding based on future revenue

    Flexible, higher cost
